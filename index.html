Swarm of Aerial Drones in Disaster Scenarios

Problem

In natural disaster scenarios, communication networks play a crucial role in the dissemination of information that can save lives. However, in many emergency situations, damage to communication infrastructure makes this exchange impossible, putting lives at risk.

These situations demonstrate that, at times when communication is most needed, it tends to fail. This highlights the need for robust solutions that ensure connectivity in emergency situations.

The aim of this project is to provide a reliable connectivity solution in such cases by using a drone swarm to establish 5G connectivity in high-risk areas.

Goals

5G: Implementing advanced 5G technology to enable high-speed communication and data transfer in affected areas.

Mesh Network: Establishing a mesh network within the drone swarm to guarantee robust and efficient connectivity to ensure that users have acess to the internet

Drone Coordination: Implementing an algorithm that efficiently coordinates the swarm's movements.
