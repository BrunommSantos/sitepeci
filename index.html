About the Project

The “UAV5G” project aims to develop solutions for the use of drones in disaster scenarios, ensuring communication and connectivity in situations of compromised infrastructure.

September - December
Project Stages

From the analysis of the state of the art to the construction and initial testing of the system.

October
System Architecture

Development of the architecture and definition of the system's technical requirements.

November
Development

Início da fase de construção, integração dos drones, implementação da rede mesh e comunicação 5G.

December
Report

Presentation of the report with the obtained results.
